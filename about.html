Who We Are

Omnori is a relentless product lab building software, automation, and productivity tools of every kind. We prototype rapidly and ship continuously, and release our work as open source under libre licenses so anyone can use it, inspect it, and build on it.

What We Build

  • Nori Cam — a Chrome extension camera app with overlays, filters, and zoom.
  • Pomori — a Chrome extension pomodoro timer.
  • Notes101 (coming soon) — a Discord bot for meeting notes and team updates.
  • Link Shortener (coming soon) — link shortening with managed, expiring redirects.

Get Involved
