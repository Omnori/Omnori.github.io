Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 25, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Omnori. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our browser extensions, including Infinity V Cam and PomoInfinity, handle user data.

2. Data Collection and Usage

We believe in minimal data collection. Our extensions are designed to work locally on your device.

Infinity V Cam

  • Camera Access: Infinity V Cam requires access to your camera to provide virtual background and video enhancement features. This video processing is performed entirely locally on your device. No video or image data is ever transmitted to our servers or any third parties.
  • Settings: We store your preferences (e.g., chosen filters, backgrounds) locally in your browser's storage.

PomoInfinity

  • Usage Data: We do not collect personal identification information. PomoInfinity may store your timer settings and productivity stats locally in your browser.

Nori scrapes images

  • Usage Data: We do not collect personal identification information. Nori scrapes images may store your timer settings and productivity stats locally in your browser.

3. Data Storage and Handling

All data processed by our extensions (such as video streams in Infinity V Cam or timer settings in PomoInfinity) is handled in real-time within your browser's memory or stored in your browser's local storage (Chrome Storage API).

We do not use external databases to store your personal information or usage history.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or trade your user data with any third parties. There are no external parties that have access to your data through our products.

5. Security

By keeping your data local to your device, we minimize the risk of data breaches. We follow industry-standard practices for browser extension development to ensure your environment remains secure.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
